Description

The most poopular lasagna recipe in Allrecipes by John Chandler. Incredibly customizable and can be altered to suit your needs!

Ingredients

  • Meat (sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products (crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste)
  • White sugar
  • Spices and seasonings (fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses (parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta)
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
